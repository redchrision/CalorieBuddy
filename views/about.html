About CalorieBuddy

CalorieBuddy is your personal application to keep track of food items, with the main aim to promote a healthy lifestyle and raising awareness about food details.

You have the possibility to add or delete foods in the database, search for food iteams, update the list as you preffer and also keep track of all its content.

The Team

The developer of CalorieBuddy is Cristina DeLisle, a big enthousiast of a healthy lifestyle and currently a student at University of London pursuing a BSc Degree in Computer Science.

She believes that by being aware of the various ingredients, you will have the possiblity to be more careful about what you are eating and, as a result, make the best food choices.
